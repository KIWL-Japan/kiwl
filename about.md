# About Us

KIWL is a volunteer charity group committed to raising funds and awareness for Mirai no Mori.

Our community supports Mirai no Mori through cycling challenges, running events, social fundraisers, and other activities that bring people together for a practical purpose: helping children access outdoor programs that build confidence and lifelong skills.

## What We Do

- Organize fundraising events and community challenges.
- Share updates about Mirai no Mori and the impact of donations.
- Encourage supporters, sponsors, and volunteers to get involved.

## Who We Support

Mirai no Mori creates outdoor learning opportunities for children in Japan. Its programs help participants discover new strengths, form positive relationships, and experience nature in a supportive environment.
