# Sponsors

## Sponsorship overview

Thanks to our generous sponsors, we have raised over ¥130 million since our humble beginnings. Without their help and support, we would not have been able to transform the lives of so many marginalized children. Many of our corporate sponsors also encourage their staff to get fit and give back by participating in or volunteering for KIWL events. It's a win-win!

## Benefits of being a sponsor

- Your company's logo on our event clothing, website, banners, and social media.
- Community contribution; helping disadvantaged children living in care homes in Japan.
- Enhancing a company's CSR image by associating with and participating in, events which encourage getting fit and giving back in the outdoors.

## Become a sponsor

There are so many ways to sponsor, including a cash or prize donation and in-kind items, or just encouraging your staff to join our events to get fit and give back. Becoming a sponsor is easy, please contact Rob Williams, KIWL Chair & Co-Founder, [info@kiwl.net](mailto:info@kiwl.net?subject=Sponsor%20Inquiry) or visit our [Contact](/contact/) page and fill out the form.
